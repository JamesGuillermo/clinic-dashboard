================================================================================
                     TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

PROBLEM: "Data not loading"
SOLUTION: Make sure your file has the correct column names listed above.

PROBLEM: "Some charts are empty"
SOLUTION: Check if your filters are too restrictive. Click "Clear All Filters".

PROBLEM: "Numbers look wrong after clearing filters"
SOLUTION: Refresh the page (press F5) and reload your data.

PROBLEM: "Can't see the sidebar on mobile"
SOLUTION: Tap the hamburger menu (‚ò∞) in the top-left corner.
